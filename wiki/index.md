- [[sources/2022-model-estimates-of-chinas-terrestrial-water-storage-variation-due-to-reservoir-operation]] - Explicitly models 3,547 reservoirs to estimate how reservoir operation shapes China's terrestrial water storage variation and seasonal water-cycle regulation.
- [[sources/2022-high-quality-reconstruction-of-chinas-natural-streamflow]] - Reconstructs monthly natural streamflow across 330 Chinese catchments for 1961 to 2018 using a coupled hydrological and routing workflow.
- [[sources/2022-high-quality-reconstruction-of-chinas-natural-streamflow-supplementary-materials]] - Adds the station-type breakdown, water-balance naturalization logic, and uncertainty-analysis details behind the main 2022 dataset.
- [[sources/2024-a-mass-conserving-perceptron-for-machine-learning-based-modeling-of-geoscientific-systems]] - Proposes a physically interpretable mass-conserving recurrent unit that bridges physical-conceptual hydrology and machine-learning rainfall-runoff modeling.
- [[sources/2024-satellite-based-precipitation-error-propagation-in-the-hydrological-modeling-chain-across-china]] - Examines how satellite precipitation uncertainty propagates into runoff and streamflow across 366 Chinese catchments.
- [[sources/2025-warming-climate-and-water-withdrawals-threaten-river-flow-connectivity-in-china]] - Uses reconstructed natural-flow modeling to assess intermittency, climate warming, and water-withdrawal impacts across 217,001 river reaches in China.
- [[sources/2025-warming-climate-and-water-withdrawals-threaten-river-flow-connectivity-in-china-supporting-information]] - Clarifies the extended 651-station workflow, UDSBC bias correction, low-flow metrics, and modified DCI behind the 2025 connectivity analysis.
- [[sources/2026-distilling-hydrological-and-land-surface-model-parameters-from-physio-geographical-properties-using-text-generating-ai]] - Uses a variational-autoencoder-based equation generator to discover interpretable hydrological parameter transfer functions that outperform manual transfer functions and a regional LSTM benchmark in ungauged-basin runoff prediction.
